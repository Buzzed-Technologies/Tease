Our Business

ThreadPay is the premier payment and discovery platform for deeply personalized AI relationship models.

01

Hyper-Realistic AI Models

We host custom AI models that are deeply trained on relationship and human interaction data. These models create extraordinarily realistic conversational experiences that evolve from friendly question-answering to deep, involved relationships with users. Monetization Platform

ThreadPay functions as a payment and discovery platform where users find and subscribe to AI companions. We handle all payment processing, content moderation, and platform infrastructure, allowing creators to focus on developing compelling AI personalities. Creator Ecosystem

We've built a robust ecosystem for AI personality creators to develop, train, and monetize their models. Creators receive comprehensive tools, infrastructure, and audience access while retaining creative control. This approach has attracted top talent to our platform, resulting in superior content that drives user engagement and retention.
